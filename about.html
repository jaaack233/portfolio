About Me

Hi there, my name is Jack. I graduated from UC San Diego in 2022 with a B.S. in Cognitive Science specified in Human-Computer Interaction. I'm currently pursuing a master's degree at University of Michigan, focusing on UX design and research.

As a designer, I always try to be a good user by paying attention to my experience while interacting with the product and provide feedback to the company. When I am doing my own designs, I will try to implement emotion design into the product because I believe a good product should always bring good emotion impact to the users.

Outside of working, I have various interests like traveling, photography, cooking, bakering, gaming, guitar, and CG drawing. I keep exploring new fields and develop new skills because to me, the meaning of life is TO EXPERIENCE, TO EXPLORE!
