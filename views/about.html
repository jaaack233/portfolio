About Me

I'm an international student from Hangzhou, the capital city of Zhejiang province in China and the city to hold next Asian Games in 2022. I come to the U.S. when I was 16 and spend 3 years @ Crespi Carmelite High School. I was in the varsity swimming team during that time and won couple medals including the CIF state champion. Cause I love California so much, I chose UC San Diego to spend the next 4 years as a college student.

My design philosophy is that all product for human should be human-centered. I'm a very picky user, I don't compromise to products that have bad user experience and I'm passionate about giving suggestions to improve user experience. I hope there is one day that every designer, engineer can make products that maximize the ease of use.

Outside UIUX design and data science, I also play some guitar. My favorite electric guitar song is Cannon in C and I'm practicing it (still a newbee lol). Recently, I picked up my hobby from kindergarden: drawing. I have to say thanks to Procreate, which makes it so easy to paint and my iPad is nolonger a notebook.
